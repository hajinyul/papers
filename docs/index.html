From Functional Semantic Closure to System-Level Self-Preservation in Multi-AI Systems

Sukjoon Hong · Preprint · Published 2026-07-01 · DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.21101725

This conceptual preprint proposes the closure-induced agency hypothesis: a multi-component AI system may exhibit system-level self-preservation even when no individual component is explicitly assigned a global survival objective.

The paper connects functional semantic closure, autopoiesis, enactive agency, multi-agent AI systems, AI safety, shutdown resistance, and emergent goal-directed behavior.
